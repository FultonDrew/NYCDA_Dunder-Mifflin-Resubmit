Products

Copy Paper

Our copy paper is the best paper on the market. We chop our own, hand-selected trees from the perfectly maintained state forests from beautiful Lackawanna county. The lumber contains a very fresh smell that we pride ourselves in keeping throughout our manufacturing process. Our founder, Michael Scott, believes that the smell of our paper helps to minimize stress and increase Libido. Just ask our co-CEO, Jan Levinson!
Napkins

The napkins that we sell here at Michael Scott Paper Company are the softest, single-ply napkin on the market. We first line each sheet of paper with felt to get that beautiful pool table feel on every wipe. The crumbs in the corners of your mouth stand no chance to the grit that these napkins possess. We proudly test each napkin against BBQ sauce, mustard and red wine before packaging and shipping them to you. This process is unrivaled from any of our competitors, especially the lower-quality paper company, Dunder Mifflin.
Notepads

Ever need a pad of paper to take notes?
Look no further than our high quality notepads. We offer all sizes, from pocket size to legal, with options to customize the line color and paper color -- all you need to do is ask! Our salespeople are so knowledgeable -- the most knowledgable in all things notepad!
